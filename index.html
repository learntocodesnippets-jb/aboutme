I'm a software engineer based in Pune, India, with a background in Java and Python, and a growing interest in AI, machine learning, and cognitive science — particularly how people form and revise beliefs.

This site is where I document programming learnings, post opinions on software practices, reflect on scripture, and occasionally write about books or research I found worthwhile.

Outside of code, I read the Bible and think about how faith and intellectual curiosity intersect. I believe good engineering and honest inquiry share the same root: a willingness to keep learning.
